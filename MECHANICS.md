# Gameplay Systems & Mechanics

## 1. Wave-Based Progression
Gameplay is structured around escalating "Waves." As the player survives, enemy density and difficulty increase, requiring better positioning and weapon management.

## 2. The Armory (Unlocking System)
* **Starting Loadout:** All players begin with the reliable `Pistol`.
* **Unlock Logic:** Performance metrics (Kills/Waves) feed into a set-based unlock system, allowing players to expand their arsenal for future runs.
* **Dynamic Stats:** The game tracks and displays active weapon sets to provide clear feedback on progression.

## 3. Scoring & Statistics
BulletZone features a robust career-tracking system:
* **High Score:** Persistent tracking of the player's best individual performance.
* **Career Milestones:** Cumulative tracking of Total Games, Total Waves, and Total Kills.
* **Global Reset:** A "Clear Progress" utility for players to reset their career data and experience the progression loop from scratch.
